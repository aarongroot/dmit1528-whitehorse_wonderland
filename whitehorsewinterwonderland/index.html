Whitehorse

Whitehorse, capital of Canada's Yukon Territory, offers the biggest range of activities, attractions, and world class food & drink.

See what you can do?

Northern Lights Viewing

Whitehorse is an excellent place to witness the Northern Lights (Aurora Borealis). Plan a trip during the winter months, and take a guided tour or head to a dark, open area to see this breathtaking natural phenomenon.

Yukon Wildlife Preserve

Get up close to the local wildlife at the Yukon Wildlife Preserve, where you can see animals like moose, bison, caribou, and more in a natural habitat setting.

Shopping and Dining

Explore Whitehorse's downtown area for unique shops, galleries, and restaurants offering a mix of local and international cuisine.
