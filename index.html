Whitehorse Winter Wonderland

Whitehorse, capital of Canada's Yukon Territory, offers the largest range of excursions, attractions, and world class food & drink. From star-gazing to immaculate hikes to a growing shopping scene Whitehorse is truly a Winter Wonderland.

Aurora Borealis. What a sight to see! Whitehorse offers exclusive tours of this breathtaking natural phenomenon.

Yukon Wildlife Preserve

Get up close to the local wildlife at the Yukon Wildlife Preserve. You can see animals like moose, bison, caribou, and even bears in their natural winter wonderland environment.
