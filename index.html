Welcome to Whitehorse Winter Wonderland

Whitehorse, capital of Canada's Yukon Territory, offers the largest range of excursions, attractions, and world class food & drink. From star-gazing, to breath-taking hikes, to a growing shopping scene Whitehorse is truly a Winter Wonderland.

See what you can do in this Winter Wonderland

Northern Lights Viewing

Aurora Borealis, what a sight to see! Whitehorse offers exclusive tours of this breathtaking natural phenomenon. Rent a cabin for the night and have a cozy night gazing up at the sky.

Yukon Winter Wildlife Preserve

Whitehorse is full of all sorts of different creatures. The Yukon Wildlife Reserve allows guests to stop by and see the animals in their natural winter wonderland. From snowy owls to grizzly bears, the Yukon Wildlife Reserve is sure to provide memorable memories.

Shopping and Dining in Whitehorse

Explore Whitehorse's downtown area for unique shops, breweries, and restaurants offering a mix of local and international cuisine from the best chefs Yukon has to offer.

Yukon Black Spruce

"Black Spruce began in 2019 with the goal of creating a sustainable and beautiful place to stay in Whitehorse. The rocky property was considered essentially undevelopable, but by working with engineers and the city of Whitehorse, Black Spruce was able to situate the cabins in the landscape, creating a peaceful forested guest experience"~ Yukon Black Spruce

These cabins are renown for their character and charm. What sets Black Spruce cabins apart is the color of the siding. Black spruce is such an apt name for these trees as the entire sidings of these cabins are completely black! This allows for the warmest cabins available. You will never be cold from Whitehorse winters as these cabins are able to hold so much heat from the beatiful Whitehorse sun.
